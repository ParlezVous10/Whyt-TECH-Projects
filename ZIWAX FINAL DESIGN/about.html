OUR STORY

ZIWAX GLOBAL RESOURCES LTD. stands as a premier division of ZIWAX GLOBAL VENTURES, having rebranded from its original name, ZIWAX GLOBAL VENTURES, to its current form in 2007. Founded in 1997 in Rivers State, Nigeria, our company began as a specialist in the supply and procurement of ICT equipment and facilities.

Today, as a leading EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction) company, ZIWAX GLOBAL RESOURCES LTD. has transformed into a powerhouse in the oil and gas sector. We are fully committed to providing top-tier equipment and facilities, leveraging our extensive expertise to deliver innovative and efficient solutions.

Our dedication to excellence is underscored by our recent ISO 9001:2015 certification, a testament to our unwavering commitment to world-class quality and service. This certification not only highlights our adherence to rigorous standards but also reaffirms our position as an industry leader.

At ZIWAX GLOBAL RESOURCES LTD., we are committed to setting new benchmarks in excellence. Each project is executed with unparalleled precision, creativity, and an unwavering commitment to delivering superior results.
