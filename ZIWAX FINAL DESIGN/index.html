Welcome to ZIWAX

ZIWAX GLOBAL RESOURCES LTD. is a forward-thinking, indigenous enterprise, established to meet the growing demand for swift, efficient, and top-tier services that adhere to both local and international standards.

Originally known as ZIWAX GLOBAL VENTURES, the company rebranded in 2007, marking a pivotal evolution in our journey.

Today, as a premier EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction) company, we’ve expanded our focus from ICT to the procurement and supply of high-quality oil and gas equipment and facilities.

We are committed to innovation, precision, and excellence, infusing every project with unmatched professionalism and creativity.

Join us in redefining industry standards — with pioneering solutions and a relentless pursuit of perfection.
